Research Interests:

My research interests focus on the stability, robustness, and control of large-scale network systems with application to power grids, traffic networks, and neural networks. Topics of interest include:
  • Stability, control, and optimization in network systems
  • Safety and robustness of neural networks and learning algorithms
  • Congestion and multistability in flow networks
  • Geometric control and controllability of systems
