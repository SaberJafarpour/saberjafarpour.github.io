Research Interests:

My research interests focus on the safety and autonomy in learning-based interconnected systems with application to power grids traffic networks and robotic systems. Topics of interest:
  • Reachability and robustness of learning-based systems
  • Stability, resilience, and control of power grids
  • Congestion and multistability in flow networks.
