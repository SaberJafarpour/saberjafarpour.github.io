Description:

This course is an introduction to control theory focusing on linear time-invariant (LTI) systems. Although we mainly study single-input single-output (SISO) systems, some of our analysis for SISO systems can be extended to multi-input multi-output (MIMO) systems. In this course, we study LTI, SISO systems in three different representations, namely, state-space, Laplace domain, and Frequency response. In each representation, different techniques for studying controllability, observability and stability of the system are developed. At the end, the notion of feedback is introduced and the role of uncertainty in stability of systems is analyzed.
