I am a Research Assistant Professor at the University of Colorado Boulder. Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Institute for Robotics and Intelligent Machines at the Georgia Institute of Technology, working with Sam Coogan, and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Center for Control, Dynamical Systems, and Computation at the University of California, Santa Barbara, working with Francesco Bullo. I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Queen's University under the supervision of Andrew Lewis. My research lies at the intersection of control theory and autonomy, with applications to robotics and cyber-physical systems. More specifically, I focus on the following research areas:
  • Safety assurance of learning-enabled systems
  • Performance of optimization and learning algorithms
  • Resilience of large-scale autonomous systems
  • Geometric control of nonlinear systems
